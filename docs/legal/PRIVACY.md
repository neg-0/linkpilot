# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LinkPilot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our website and services.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Name (optional)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe)

### Website Data
When you analyze a website, we temporarily process:
- Page URLs from your sitemap
- Page content (titles, headings, body text)
- Existing internal link structure

**Important:** We do not permanently store your raw page content. Content is processed to generate AI embeddings, then discarded. We retain only:
- URLs analyzed
- Link suggestions generated
- Analysis metadata (timestamps, page counts)

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type
- Pages visited on LinkPilot
- Features used
- Error logs

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve LinkPilot services
- Process payments
- Send service-related emails
- Respond to support requests
- Analyze usage patterns (aggregated, anonymized)

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your website content for AI training
- Share individual data without consent

---

## Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure
- **Analysis results:** Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
- **Payment records:** Retained as required by law (typically 7 years)

---

## Data Sharing

We share data only with:
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Plausible:** Privacy-friendly analytics (no cookies, no personal data)
- **Sentry:** Error tracking (anonymized)

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

---

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption
- Secure password hashing
- Regular security audits
- Access controls for employee data access
