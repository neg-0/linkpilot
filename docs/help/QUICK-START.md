# LinkPilot Quick Start Guide

Get your first internal link suggestions in under 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Find Your Sitemap URL

Your sitemap is usually at one of these locations:
- `https://yoursite.com/sitemap.xml`
- `https://yoursite.com/sitemap_index.xml`
- `https://yoursite.com/post-sitemap.xml`

**Not sure?** Check your site's `robots.txt` file ‚Äî it often lists the sitemap location.

---

## Step 2: Paste & Analyze

1. Go to [LinkPilot](/)
2. Paste your sitemap URL in the input field
3. Click **Analyze Site**

LinkPilot will:
- Crawl your pages (up to 50 in free tier)
- Extract content and existing links
- Generate AI embeddings for semantic matching
- Find link opportunities

This usually takes 1-3 minutes depending on your site size.

---

---

## Step 4: Export & Implement

Click **Export CSV** to download your action plan.

The CSV includes:
- Source URL
- Target URL
- Suggested anchor text
- Similarity score

Open in Google Sheets or Excel, then work through the list. Most sites can implement 20-50 links in a weekend.

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start with high-score suggestions** (0.8+) ‚Äî these are the most relevant
2. **Prioritize orphan pages** ‚Äî they need love
3. **Use natural anchor text** ‚Äî don't over-optimize
4. **Re-run monthly** ‚Äî as you add content, new opportunities appear
